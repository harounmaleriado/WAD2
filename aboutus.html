About Us

EcoTechHavan is dedicated to promoting sustainability and reducing electronic waste by providing high-quality second-hand PC parts and components. We believe in the importance of recycling and reusing electronics to minimize environmental impact and conserve valuable resources.

Benefits of Recycling Second-Hand Components

  • Reduces electronic waste and landfill pollution.
  • Conserves valuable raw materials and energy.
  • Promotes a circular economy, reducing the need for new production.
  • Provides affordable and eco-friendly options for consumers.

The Harms of E-Waste

Electronic waste (e-waste) contains hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium, which can pollute soil and water, posing serious health risks to humans and wildlife. By recycling e-waste, we can prevent these harmful substances from contaminating our environment.
