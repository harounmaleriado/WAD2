Earn Points

When you actively participate in HARD CYCLE Support Communities, you can earn points and get recognition for sharing quality content. Here’s what you can do to earn points and how many points you’ll get.

Activity Points
Benchmark Score Upload: Users earn points for uploading benchmark scores of computer components. 3
Post or reply to threads: Users earn points when they post/reply to threads in the community. 2
Leaderboard Rankings: Users earn points based on their position in the leaderboards for different product categories or overall community rankings. 1




Level Up

When you actively participate in HARD CYCLE Support Communities, you can earn points and get recognition for sharing quality content. Here’s what you can do to earn points and how many points you’ll get.

Level Points Benefits
1 0 Start your journey by participating in discussions, asking questions, and helping others.
2 1000 Reward 500 points
3 3000 Reward 1000 points




Get on the leaderboards

Share your expertise and helpful answers in your favorite product areas to get recognition on the leaderboards in HARD CYCLE Support Communities. Leaderboards showcase the highest ranking members based on the points that they've earned in each product community.
